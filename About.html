About Me

I am a second year undergraduate student at the University of California, San Diego working in organic synthesis. After graduating from Santa Monica High School, I entered UCSD as a marine biology major working in a cellular biology lab studying protein breakdown.


After switching my major to biochemistry to provide myself with a broader experience in discovering my interests, I joined the Gerwick Lab working under Dr. Nicole Avalon. My main tasks in this position were preparing isolated biological fractions for instrumental analysis and preparing the lab's growth media for the cyanobacteria cultures.


In the summer of 2022, I transitioned to working under Dr. Jehad Almaliti to pursue my interests in organic chemistry as a molecular synthesis major. Since then, I have been working on a project conducting peptide coupling reactions for the production of small-molecule therapeutics.
